How to Play Spades

🎯 Objective

Be the first team to reach 500 points. You play with a partner (North) against two opponents (East & West).

🃏 The Deal

All 52 cards are dealt equally — 13 cards to each player. Spades (♠) are always trump.

📊 Bidding

Each player bids the number of tricks they expect to win. The team's total bid is what you must achieve.

Nil Bid: Bid 0 tricks. If you succeed: +100 pts. If you fail: -100 pts.

Blind Nil (Expert): Bid nil before looking at cards. Success: +200, Fail: -200.

🏆 Scoring

  • Made bid: 10 × bid + 1 per overtrick (bag)
  • Failed bid: −10 × bid
  • Bags: Every 10 bags = −100 pts penalty

🎮 Playing Tricks

  • You must follow the led suit if possible
  • Spades cannot lead until "broken" (a spade has been played)
  • Highest card of the led suit wins, unless a spade is played
  • Highest spade wins when spades are played
